Zucchini Bread

Description

Delight your senses with the wholesome charm of Zucchini Bread. Imagine a moist, tender loaf, infused with the earthy sweetness of freshly grated zucchini, and speckled with crunchy bites of walnuts or pecans. Each slice offers a symphony of flavors and textures, with hints of warm cinnamon, vanilla, and nutmeg mingling perfectly with the subtle freshness of the zucchini. Whether enjoyed as a comforting breakfast treat, a satisfying snack, or a sweet ending to any meal, this timeless classic captures the essence of home-baked goodness. With its golden crust and moist interior, Zucchini Bread is not just a delicious indulgence, but a heartwarming reminder of simple joys and the abundance of nature's bounty.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 Â¼ cups white sugar
  • 3 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups grated zucchini
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

    • Step 1

      Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease and flour two 8x4-inch pans.

    Step 2

      Sift flour, salt, baking powder, baking soda, and cinnamon together in a large bowl.

    Step 3

      Beat eggs, oil, sugar, and vanilla together in a separate large bowl with an electric mixer until combined; add flour mixture and beat well. Stir in zucchini and walnuts until well combined. Pour batter into the prepared pans.

    Step 4

      Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 40 to 60 minutes. Cool in the pans on a wire rack for 20 minutes. Run a table knife around the edges to loosen. Invert carefully onto a wire rack and let cool completely.
